# No Man's Sky Interactive Guide — Content

## Mission Progression Guide

### Primary Missions (Story)

#### 1. Allein inmitten der Sterne (Artemis Path)
- **Priority:** ⭐⭐⭐ MAIN STORY — do this first!
- **What:** The main storyline following Artemis, the explorer
- **Unlocks:** Portals, new galaxies, most game features
- **Current sub-quest:** "Artemis, der Entdecker"
- **Tip:** This drives progression the most. Focus here.

#### 2. Die Weltraumanomalie (Space Anomaly)
- **Priority:** ⭐⭐⭐ Parallel to Artemis
- **What:** Introduces the Nexus (multiplayer hub), Nada & Polo
- **Unlocks:** Blueprints, multiplayer missions, Nexus shop
- **Current sub-quest:** "Jenseits des Systems?"
- **Tip:** Runs parallel to Artemis Path. Visit the Anomaly when prompted.

#### 3. Der Weg des Atlas (Atlas Path)
- **Priority:** ⭐⭐ Secondary story
- **What:** Visit Atlas Interfaces, craft Atlas Seeds
- **Unlocks:** New star types (red/green/blue systems), lore
- **Current sub-quest:** "Der Staub der Schöpfung"
- **Tip:** Do this alongside Artemis when it sends you to an Atlas station.

### Secondary Missions

#### Zusammenbau der Korvette (Corvette Assembly)
- **Priority:** ⭐⭐ Cool side project
- **What:** Build your own walkable ship with interior!
- **From:** Voyagers Update
- **How:** Collect Structural Corvette Modules from salvage, pirate battles, frigate expeditions, crashed freighters
- **Current sub-quest:** "Entwurf eines bewohnbaren Raumschiffs"

#### Unter dem Rebellenstern (Under a Rebel Star)
- **Priority:** ⭐ Optional storyline
- **What:** Pirate/Outlaw storyline
- **Current sub-quest:** "Wider die rote Maschine"
- **Tip:** Fun but doesn't advance main progression much

#### Basiscomputer-Archive (Base Computer Archives)
- **Priority:** ⭐⭐ Useful
- **What:** Unlock base building blueprints over time
- **Tip:** Progress resets on a timer — check back periodically

#### Erkundungsanleitung (Exploration Guide)
- **Priority:** ⭐ Tutorial
- **What:** Basic exploration tutorials
- **Current sub-quest:** "Lokales Informationsregister"

#### Stilvolle Bilder (Stylish Pictures)
- **Priority:** ⭐ Optional/Fun
- **What:** Photo mode missions
- **Current sub-quest:** "Fotos von Landschaften machen"

## Money Making Methods

### 1. Chlorine + Oxygen Refiner Trick 🏆 (BEST)
**Requirements:** Medium or Large Refiner
**Recipe:** 1 Chlorine + 2 Oxygen = 6 Chlorine
**Method:**
1. Buy a small amount of Chlorine at any space station
2. Buy LOTS of Oxygen (cheap, available everywhere)
3. Put 1 Chlorine + 2 Oxygen in Medium Refiner
4. Get 6 Chlorine out → repeat!
5. Sell Chlorine stacks for ~600 Units each

**Profit:** Millions in 30 minutes!

### 2. Ship Scrapping 💰
**Method:**
1. Go to space station
2. Buy cheap ships from NPCs
3. Scrap them at the ship scrapper terminal
4. Sell the scrap materials + get upgrade modules
5. Rinse and repeat

**Bonus:** Also gives you ship upgrade modules to sell for Nanites!

### 3. Metal Scrap Refining ⚙️
- Pugneum → Gold/Silver in Refiner
- Sell Gold/Silver at Galactic Trade Terminal
- Good passive income alongside combat

### 4. Activated Indium Farm 🏭 (Endgame)
- Requires blue star system access (Indium Drive)
- Build mineral extractors on Activated Indium deposits
- Passive millions while you do other stuff
- Takes initial setup time but prints money forever

### 5. Ancient Bones / Salvageable Scrap 🦴
- Scan planets from space — look for "Ancient Bones" or "Salvageable Scrap"
- Land, use scanner, dig them up
- Some bones worth 1-2 million each!

## Ships

### How to Get New Ships
1. **Buy/Trade at Space Stations:** NPCs land constantly with ships. Walk up, interact, buy or trade.
2. **Buy/Trade at Trading Posts:** More ships land simultaneously = more choices
3. **Free Freighter:** After ~5 hyperspace warps, a space battle triggers. Save the freighter captain → get it FREE!
4. **Crashed Ships:** Find on planets, repair and claim (free but needs repairs)
5. **Corvette:** Build your own via the "Zusammenbau der Korvette" quest line

### Essential Tech to Install
1. **Scanner Upgrades (S-class)** — Huge bonus for scanning flora/fauna (money!)
2. **Hazard Protection Upgrades** — Survive extreme planets
3. **Hyperdrive Upgrades** — Reach further systems
4. **Mining Beam Upgrades** — Mine faster
5. **Jetpack Upgrades** — Better mobility

### Useful Keybinds / Tips
- **Scan everything!** With S-class scanner upgrades, each creature scan = 100k-400k Units
- **Upload discoveries** at the Anomaly for Nanites
- **Check the Galactic Trade Terminal** prices — buy low in one system, sell high in another
- **Signal Booster** finds nearby points of interest
- **Save often** — at save points or your ship
